      How to play

      Description

      You have some skin. You can replace this skin with another one, with a price higher in 1.5, 2, 5 or 10 times. The higher the price of a new skin, the lower the probability of success.
      If the upgrade is successful, the old skin is replaced with a new one. If it fails, the old skin disappears.
